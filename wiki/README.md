PhizMusic is a foundational music theory framework that derives every concept from physics, mathematics, and neuroscience. Where conventional Western music theory relies on 18th-century terminology and notation conventions that obscure the underlying physics, PhizMusic uses physics-literal vocabulary, transparent mathematical relationships, and psychoacoustic evidence to explain how music works and why it sounds the way it does.

Every culture's music system is a different engineering solution to the same mathematical constraints. PhizMusic provides the shared physics vocabulary to understand them all.
